Stainless Steel

Built to outlast.

Aerospace-grade T304 stainless steel, mandrel-bent and fully TIG-welded. A deeper, fuller note, free-flowing bends, and corrosion resistance that shrugs off years of heat cycles.

  • Mandrel-bent T304 stainless for uninterrupted flow
  • Hand TIG-welded seams with polished tips
  • Corrosion-resistant — built to outlast the car

Bolt-On Power

Power you keep.

Free-flowing mandrel bends and optimised back-pressure release trapped horsepower across the rev range — no ECU tune required for the gains, and no compromise on drivability.
